Why do we build?
We build to find out what the web can feel like. Every project starts as a question — can a page flow like water, can particles fall into place like a thought, can a cat help you read the market? Chasing those questions is what keeps our craft sharp and our work worth looking at twice.
Our approach
We work small, fast, and hands-on. Design and engineering share the same desk, so an idea can travel from a rough shader to a shipped product without losing its spark. We prototype in the open, keep whatever feels alive, and cut everything that doesn't.
Why these experiments?
The experiments are where we play. They let us test new techniques, break our own habits, and bank the tricks that later power our apps — like CatInsight — and the work we do for others. And honestly, they're just fun. That energy tends to end up in everything we ship.
